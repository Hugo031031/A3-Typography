Community

In the park, the community is shaped by everyday people food vendors setting up their stalls, tailors working carefully by hand, spa workers offering quiet moments of care, and many other small services. Each person brings a different rhythm and purpose to the space, yet they coexist naturally within it with continuity and care. Through daily activities and brief conversations, these individual roles come together to form a connected collective that gives the park its warmth and a strong sense of belonging.
