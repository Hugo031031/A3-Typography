In the late afternoon, the park becomes a familiar gathering place where food quietly shapes the atmosphere. Light meals, yogurt cups, and simple snacks are eaten slowly on benches or shared while walking beneath the trees. The routine feels unplanned yet ingrained, turning ordinary flavors into a steady presence.

Over time, these repeated afternoons settle into memory for the people who return here. Children grow up coming to the park, snacks in hand, while adults revisit the same paths years later with the same habits. What seems casual at first becomes nostalgic, as shared food and shared time transform the park into a place of lasting emotional comfort.
